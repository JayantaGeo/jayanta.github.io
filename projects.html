Integrated Approaches for Flood risk assessment and management strategies

Role: Host Scientist | Funding: DST, Government of India

This project develops GIS-based data-driven flood hazard prediction maps to support risk-informed decision-making. Advanced modeling techniques will be applied to assess and map flood-prone areas, with model accuracy evaluated using diverse performance metrics. Under the IPCC vulnerability assessment framework, the study will measure livelihood vulnerability in high-risk zones, capturing socio-economic, environmental, and infrastructure-related impacts. The integrated approach aims to provide scientific insights and actionable strategies for flood risk management, community resilience building, and sustainable development in flood-affected regions.
